# 📄 Problem Statement Document

## 🩺 Problem Title
**Medication Reminder and Adherence Problem**

---

## 📌 Problem Description
Many people forget to take their medicine on time or take it in the wrong way. This usually happens because of busy lifestyles or confusing medication schedules.  

Some people need to take multiple medicines at different times during the day, which makes it harder to manage properly. Because of this, their health condition may become worse, and the treatment may not work effectively.

---

## 🎯 Target Users
- People with long-term illnesses (e.g., diabetes, blood pressure)
- Elderly people who take multiple medications
- Busy individuals who forget medication timings
- Caregivers who manage medication for patients

---

## ⚙️ Current Solutions
- Phone alarms and reminder apps  
- Written notes or pill boxes  
- Basic health apps with reminder features  

---

## ❌ Why Current Solutions Fail
- Not user-friendly, especially for elderly users  
- Cannot handle complex medication schedules  
- No proper tracking of taken or missed doses  
- Reminders are easy to ignore  
- Not specifically designed for healthcare needs  

---

## 💡 Proposed Solution
We propose developing a **web-based medication reminder system** to help users manage their medicines effectively.

### Key Features:
- Add medicines with dosage and timing  
- Set reminder notifications  
- Mark medicines as taken or missed  
- View daily medication schedule in a dashboard  

The main goal is to create a **simple, user-friendly, and effective system** that helps users follow their medication properly and improve their health outcomes.
