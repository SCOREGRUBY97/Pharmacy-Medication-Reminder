## Market Opportunity
- Existing apps are too complex, especially for elderly users  
- Lack of proper tracking (taken vs missed medicine)  
- Poor handling of complex medication schedules  
- Users often feel confused or overwhelmed  
- Opportunity to build a simple and effective solution  

---

## Unique Value Proposition
- Simple and user-friendly medication reminder system  
- Clear scheduling for better understanding  
- Tracks whether medicine is taken or missed  
- Dashboard view for daily medication plan  
- Helps improve medication adherence
